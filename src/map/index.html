Bitcoin Production by Country: Choropleth

The following visualization shows, from between September 2019 and April 2020, which countries produced the largest amount of Bitcoin. Specifically, we are measuring each country's percentage share of the Bitcoin hashrate for that month. This visualization should show where Bitcoin development is concentrated, and how that has changed over time; additionally, the full picture should demonstrate the overall growth of Bitcoin.
